About Us

Who We Are

CC Site Services is a family run building company based in Cork.

CC Site Services builders specialize in all sectors of the building industry including residential new builds of single and multi-housing, residential house extensions and renovations, commercial and industrial sector build works.

Our reputation is our most important asset and during our time we have established a name for professionalism, knowledge and reliability, having successfully completed a wide range of building projects throughout Cork City & County, and nationwide, for many satisfied clients.

What We Do

CC Site Services Cork have over twenty years experience in the construction industry and have gained recognition in Cork for their superior work.

We offer competitive prices coupled with outstanding workmanship from qualified builders.

CC Site Services use only the best materials available for the job, and each job is tailored for that individual project, from loft conversions to house extensions to new builds.

Past projects have included residential and commercial building, attic conversions, extensions and renovations.

Why Choose Us

CC Site Services builders offer you the best combination of management skills and craftsmanship, giving you excellent value for money and first class quality of service.

Our extensive experience in building will guarantee you a professional clean service.

We are available to tender for your next project.
